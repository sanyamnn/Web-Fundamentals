Maruti Suzuki India Limited, formerly known as Maruti Udyog Limited, is an automobile manufacturer in India.[9] It is a 56.21%-owned subsidiary of Suzuki and motorcycle manufacturer Suzuki Motor Corporation.[8] As of January 2017, it had a market share of 51% of the Indian passenger car market.[10] Maruti Suzuki manufactures and sells popular cars such as the Ciaz, Ertiga, Wagon R, Alto, Swift, Celerio, Swift Dzire, Omni, Baleno and Baleno RS, Ignis.[11] The company is headquartered at New Delhi.[3] In February 2012, the company sold its ten millionth vehicle in India.[12]
